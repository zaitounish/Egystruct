About Egystruct

Egystruct is a construction company with a continous ambition to providing people with opportunities to improve their lives, this is our unified goal from the beginning. We have successfully acheived an accurate part of this vision, and helped launching more than 20,000 houses, and more than 5000 facilities for government, corporations, and people in different governrates across all of Egypt, and our vision is continuing to improve being applied wider, and crossing noticably in the Middle East region recently. We are continuing to have our consistent spirit and our unified goal more effectively than ever.

Egystruct Evolution

  • 2006

    First office launched in Alexandria by Ali Mohamed from Cairo University, and Sarah Helgason from Alexandria University.

  • 2010

    Company receives its first financial investment from Egyptian National Agency for Business Support.

  • 2012

    First enterprise customer: Egyptain Space Agency needing a construction of its new Head Quarters.

  • 2015

    Egystruct in international main stream news for the first time, after the American Havana Holdings fails to aquire the company.

  • 2016

    Egystruct launches service via website.

  • 2017

    Egystruct expands it offices to serve in more than 15 different governrates in Egypt.

  • 2018

    Egystruct's net worth officially reaches 2 billion US Dollars, Officially holding the title of the biggest construction company in North Africa.

  • 2021

    Egystruct launches its first, technologically prepared remote consulting service that is fully online, Due to Covid-19 outbreak.
